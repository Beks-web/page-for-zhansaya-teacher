Karakhan Mausoleum is an architectural monument located in the central part of the city of Taraz, at the intersection of two streets - Baizak batyr and Tole bi. The mausoleum is part of a cult and memorial complex formed on the territory of a medieval settlement.

The mausoleum of babaja Khatun (XI century) is located near the mausoleum of Aisha Bibi, on a high rise. The mausoleum is very simple and strict in its composition and structure. The construction is built of fired bricks.
